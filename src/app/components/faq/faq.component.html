Is Dots free?
Yes! Our goal is to give everyone access to connecting their brands with influencers, so you can use Dots free of charge.

How do I know an influencer is authentic?
Authenticity is one of the hardest things to measure. Dots is trying to crack the code by measuring influencers' engagment rates and past brand deals to provide brands with the most trustworthy influencers in the market. With that in mind, our platform aims to only suggest influencers with authentic track records.

Can I make more than one profile?
Sure! You can make both an influencer and brand profile if you would like.
